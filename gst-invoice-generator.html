Mandatory Fields in a Valid GST Bill

According to the Goods and Services Tax Act, a valid Tax Invoice must contain:

  • Supplier's Name, Address, and GSTIN
  • Invoice Number (Max 16 characters) and Date
  • Buyer's Name, Address, and GSTIN (if registered)
  • Item Description, HSN Code, Quantity, and Value
  • Applicable Tax Rates and Tax Amounts clearly separated.
