This free online calculator solves that problem instantly.

How to Check Government Scheme Eligibility Online?

  • Select your State: Choose 'All India' for central schemes (like PM Kisan) or your specific state (like Madhya Pradesh for Ladli Behna Yojana).
  • Enter Demographics: Provide your accurate age and gender, as many schemes are exclusively for women, senior citizens, or youth.
