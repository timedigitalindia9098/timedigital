💡 How Logistics Companies Charge You

Ever wondered why courier charges are sometimes higher than expected? Logistics companies in India calculate cost based on **whichever weight is higher**:

  1. Actual Weight: The actual scale reading of your parcel.
  2. Volumetric (Dimensional) Weight: The space your parcel occupies inside the truck or airplane.

The Golden Formula:

Volumetric Weight (Kg) = (L × W × H in cm) / 5000

Our automated tool compares this algorithm across India's top courier services to save you from hidden platform fees.

Understanding Volumetric vs. Actual Weight Overhead

If you are shipping light objects in large boxes (like stuffed pillows or plastic containers), courier agents will bill you using Dimensional Weight rather than structural gravity. Q1: Which is the cheapest courier service for Meesho sellers in India?
Ans: For small and medium-scale e-commerce resellers, India Post (Speed Post) provides the most competitive pricing framework for rural codes, while commercial partners like Delhivery Surface offer optimized digital automation APIs for urban pin codes.
Q2: Why do courier costs differ between surface and air delivery formats?
Ans: Surface logistics utilizes ground trucks and multi-axle freight networks which takes 4-7 business days but is highly budget friendly. Express air shipments reserve cargo hold capacity in flight structures, commanding a premium price matrix for 24-48 hour delivery.
Q3: Does this estimation sheet include GST parameters?
