💡 How Logistics Companies Charge You

Ever wondered why courier charges are sometimes higher than expected? Logistics companies in India calculate cost based on **whichever weight is higher**:

  1. Actual Weight: The actual scale reading of your parcel.
  2. Volumetric (Dimensional) Weight: The space your parcel occupies inside the truck or airplane.

The Golden Formula:

Volumetric Weight (Kg) = (L × W × H in cm) / 5000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Which is the cheapest courier service for Meesho sellers in India?
Ans: For small and medium-scale e-commerce resellers, India Post (Speed Post) provides the most competitive pricing framework for rural codes, while commercial partners like Delhivery Surface offer optimized courier charges for urban pin codes.
Q2: Why do courier costs differ between surface and air delivery formats?
Ans: Surface logistics utilizes ground trucks which takes 4-7 business days but is highly budget friendly. Express air shipments reserve cargo hold capacity in flight structures, commanding a premium shipping rate for 24-48 hour delivery.
Q3: Does this shipping cost estimator include GST parameters?
Ans: No, the base calculation matrix maps out operational cargo costs. You must add standard 18% Integrated Goods and Services Tax (IGST) over the output data for final legal computations.
Q4: What are FOV charges in logistics?
Ans: FOV stands for Freight on Value. It is an additional risk surcharge levied by courier companies on high-value shipments (like electronics or jewelry) to cover insurance during transit.
Q5: How to calculate bulk courier charges?
Ans: For shipments above 10-15 kg, commercial carriers offer discounted bulk freight rates which are significantly lower than standard retail per kg courier charges. You will need to contact the B2B division of carriers directly for accurate bulk pricing.
